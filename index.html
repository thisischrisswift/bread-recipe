Light Bread with Thin Crust

Updated for 2026: Whole Wheat, SAF Instant Yeast & Stand Mixer

Start Baking

A modernized approach to whole wheat baking. Expect a nuttier flavor, complex crumb, and that signature thin, crispy crust using a Dutch oven method.

Ingredients

  • 350g Bread Flour (70%)
  • 150g Whole Wheat Flour (30%)
  • 10g Salt (2%)
  • 4g SAF Instant Yeast (Blue Label)
  • 20g Honey (Optional)
  • 400g Water (80% Hydration)

Equipment

  • Stand Mixer (Dough Hook)
  • 5qt Dutch Oven
  • Digital Scale
  • Proofing Basket / Cloth-lined Bowl
  • Instant-read Thermometer

Method

01

Autolyse

45 mins

Combine both flours and 400g water in stand mixer. Mix on low until just incorporated. Cover and rest.

Do not add salt or yeast yet.
02

Mix

10 mins

Add salt, yeast, and honey. Mix speed 2 (3-4 mins), then speed 4 (5-6 mins). Dough should clear sides but stick to bottom.

03

Bulk Fermentation

1.5 - 2 hrs

Transfer to oiled bowl. Cover. After 45 mins, perform one set of stretch-and-folds. Rest until 50-75% volume increase.

Target Temp: 68°F. Watch the dough, not the clock.
04

Shape & Proof

1 - 2 hrs

Shape into round boule, creating tension. Place in proofing basket seam-side up. Proof at room temp.

Test: Poke dough. Slow spring back = Ready.

05

Preheat

30 mins

Place Dutch oven in oven. Preheat to 475°F (245°C). Heat for 30 mins.

06

Score & Bake

40 mins

Score dough on parchment. Load into Dutch oven.

  • Phase 1: Lid ON, 475°F for 20 mins.
  • Phase 2: Lid OFF, 425°F for 15-20 mins.

Done when internal temp reaches 205-207°F.

07

Cool

1 hr

Cool completely on wire rack before slicing.

Baker's Notes

Flour & Texture

Expect a slightly denser crumb and nuttier flavor due to whole wheat. Gluten strands are cut by bran, so volume might be slightly less than white bread.

Hydration (80%)

Whole wheat absorbs more water. If unmanageable, add 10-20g bread flour. Wetter dough = better texture.

Yeast

SAF Instant (Blue) needs no blooming. Add directly to flour.
